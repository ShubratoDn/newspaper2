After 30 days, the country saw death in Corona

Shubrato Debnath

Published: 21 May 2022, 17:59

One person has died of corona virus in the last 24 hours in the country. After 30 days in a row, the country saw death in Corona. The last one died on April 20.

This information has been given in the regular bulletin about corona sent from the Department of Health on Saturday (May 20).

Meanwhile, in 24 hours, samples of 3,926 people were tested and corona was identified in the bodies of 18 people. In contrast to the test, the detection rate is zero point 41 percent. So far 19 lakh 53 thousand 204 corona have been identified in the country. 162 people have recovered in the last 24 hours. With this, a total of 19 lakh 747 people recovered. A total of 29,126 people have died in Corona in the country.

At the end of 2019, the first corona infection was detected in Wuhan, China. Within a few months, the virus spread throughout the world. The first corona was identified in the country on March 6, 2020. The first death occurred on March 17 of the same year.
